1Market Problem

Early-stage growth tools lose momentum when distribution is inconsistent. The core issue is not AI quality alone; it is execution consistency and channel discipline.

  • Founders know they should post, but execution drops.
  • Teams lack a repeatable visibility workflow.
  • Competitors win attention by showing up consistently.

2Strategic Positioning

Position MeetMeg as a consistency engine, not only a content generator.

Message: “Consistency creates authority. Authority creates pipeline.”
  • Customer: founder/operator with low content consistency.
  • Problem: no repeatable system.
  • Outcome: visible brand + inbound momentum.

3ICP Focus

  • Primary: Agency owners + consultants
  • Secondary: B2B founders (1–50 employees)
  • Tertiary: Fractional executives

Reason: these segments already value visibility and can adopt faster with clearer ROI narrative.

4Channel & Distribution Motion

  • Primary channel: LinkedIn (operator-led distribution)
  • Apollo-assisted targeting for outbound precision
  • Flow: Content → Comment/DM → Diagnosis → Demo

590-Day Rollout

  • Phase 1 (Weeks 1–2): Message + ICP calibration
  • Phase 2 (Weeks 3–6): Channel activation + cadence
  • Phase 3 (Weeks 7–12): Conversion optimization + scale

Weekly KPI reviews guide message refinement and segment prioritization.

6KPI Framework

  • Impressions and engagement quality
  • Conversations started
  • Demos booked
  • Activation rate

This is the operational scorecard for GTM fit and channel efficiency.
