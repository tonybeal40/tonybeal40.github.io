15-Minute Call Sheet

Mark Hupe — Discovery Call

Keep this simple. Ask enough to find the bottleneck, mirror it back clearly, then close toward a short audit / tightening engagement.

Open with this

“Mark, from everything you’ve shared, it sounds like the foundation is already there. What I want to understand is where the process is losing momentum right now, and what would need to tighten up for it to produce more consistently. Then I can show you how I’d approach tightening it.”

Ask these first

  1. Of the searches you’ve already built, which ones feel like they’re pulling the right physicians most consistently?
  2. Where do you feel the targeting is strongest right now, and where does it still feel too broad?
  3. In the question-led outreach you’re already using, where do you feel it’s working best right now?
  4. Where do you feel the messaging is creating interest, but not consistently creating the next conversation?
  5. How are you thinking about follow-up right now after the first response or non-response?
  6. Once someone engages on LinkedIn, how are you currently moving that person into CLEARpath?
  7. If you look at the process end to end, where do you think the biggest drop-off is happening today?

If you need to go deeper

  1. Are there certain physician segments that seem more responsive or more aligned than others?
  2. When a physician does respond, what kinds of responses are you seeing most often?
  3. Do you feel the issue is mostly first-message performance, or more what happens after someone engages?
  4. At what point does the LinkedIn → CLEARpath handoff feel clean, and at what point does it start to feel messy?
  5. Are you using CLEARpath right now more as a true next-step pipeline, or more as a place to log activity?
  6. If we tightened one part first, where do you think the biggest immediate gain would come from?
  7. If this were working the way you want, what would be happening more consistently than it is now?

What you’re listening for

  • Reply rate
  • Conversation quality
  • Main drop-off point
  • Whether audience segments are mixed together
  • Whether the next step is clear enough to convert
  • Whether CLEARpath is a working pipeline or just a record

Best follow-up prompts

  • “What makes you say that?”
  • “Where do you think that starts?”
  • “Is that a targeting issue, a messaging issue, or a process issue?”
  • “What happens next after that?”
  • “Where does it break?”

Mirror back

“What I’m hearing is the foundation is already there. The bigger opportunity now is tightening where the process loses momentum — making sure the right physician segments are being worked the right way, the follow-up is structured, and the handoff into CLEARpath is clean enough that good conversations actually turn into movement.”

Light strategy only

  • “This isn’t a volume game. It’s about asking the right question that earns the second question.”
  • “No real conversations should live only in LinkedIn.”
  • “Personalization here is more about context than fake custom lines.”
  • “Different physician stages care about different things.”

Do not do this

  • Don’t teach the whole system live
  • Don’t over-explain tooling
  • Don’t overclaim physician-finance expertise
  • Don’t slip into freelancer/task language
  • Position yourself as the guy who fixes bottlenecks and revenue flow

Close with this

“What I’d suggest is a short working engagement where I come in, audit what you’ve already built, tighten the messaging, follow-up structure, and how the process flows into CLEARpath, then build the next phase around what is actually producing traction. Does that feel like the right direction?”

If he asks about the first 30 days

  • Week 1: audit only
  • Week 2: tighten segmentation and message structure
  • Week 3: run small controlled tests
  • Week 4: review movement and tighten bottlenecks

Keep it high-level. Do not map the whole system in detail on the call.
