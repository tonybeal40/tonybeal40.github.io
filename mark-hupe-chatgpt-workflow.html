ChatGPT Workflow — Use It to Sharpen Expertise

The goal is not to let ChatGPT write generic copy for you. The goal is to use it to help you clarify segment differences, surface patterns, organize objections, and turn your real expertise into cleaner messaging and process structure.

Back to discovery call page

Use case 1 — Compare physician segments

Prompt: Compare these physician segments as retirement-income planning prospects: dermatologists, cardiologists, orthopedic surgeons, radiologists, and anesthesiologists. For each, explain likely income profile, likely planning complexity, likely pain points, likely objections, and what kind of trust-based message angle would resonate most. Keep it practical and avoid generic marketing language.

Use case 2 — Organize objections

Prompt: I am helping physicians with retirement-income planning, tax efficiency, family protection, and durable retirement income. Here are the objections and hesitations I hear most often: [paste notes]. Organize these into categories, identify the beliefs underneath them, and show me how to answer them in a calm, trust-based way without sounding pushy.

Use case 3 — Improve message clarity

Prompt: Here is how I currently explain what I do: [paste your wording]. Rewrite this 3 ways: 1. clearer for a physician close to retirement 2. clearer for a practice owner 3. clearer for a high-income physician who has savings but no real income plan Keep the tone thoughtful, question-led, and trust-based.

Use case 4 — Turn raw notes into a process

Prompt: Here are my rough notes about how I want LinkedIn outreach to move into UpReach: [paste notes]. Turn this into a simple step-by-step process with stages, decision points, and next actions. Keep it simple enough to use in real life.

Use case 5 — Pull insight from real client language

Prompt: Here are phrases, concerns, and questions my physician clients have used: [paste examples]. Identify the recurring themes, emotional drivers, and trust triggers. Then tell me what this suggests about how I should position the next conversation.
