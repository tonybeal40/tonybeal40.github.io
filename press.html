Tony Beal is a Revenue Operations leader and AI sales systems builder with 15+ years of B2B experience across manufacturing, pharmaceutical, food & beverage, and industrial markets. Based in St. Louis, Missouri, Tony specializes in CRM architecture, AI-powered outreach automation, and pipeline management frameworks that create predictable, repeatable revenue systems.

He has managed 3,700+ accounts, built a $6M+ post-sale program from scratch, and generated $15M+ in pipeline while maintaining 8 consecutive years on quota. His proprietary AI system — built on 40,000+ engineered prompts — enables enterprise-scale personalized outreach run by a single operator.

Tony writes about RevOps, AI in B2B sales, and pipeline architecture. He publishes The Results Brief, a monthly LinkedIn newsletter with 1,400+ subscribers and 37,000+ annual impressions. He is available for contract and full-time Revenue Operations engagements.
