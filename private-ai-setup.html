Common Questions

How much does the hardware cost?

For a 5–15 person team, a capable mini PC runs $400–$900. Larger teams or heavier workloads may need a $1,500–$3,000 server. I'll give you exact recommendations on the call.

Is it as good as ChatGPT?

For most business tasks — drafting, summarizing, answering questions — yes. For cutting-edge research or image generation, ChatGPT still leads. But for day-to-day business use, local models like Llama 3 and Mistral are excellent.

Do you work remotely or onsite?

Both. I can set up and configure everything remotely for most installations. For St. Louis and Metro East Illinois clients, I'm available onsite.

What if something breaks?

I provide documentation and basic support after setup. For ongoing support contracts, we can discuss options on the call.
