Homework Before We Start

This is not busywork. The goal is to sharpen who you want, what they care about, and how your expertise gets turned into a repeatable process before outreach begins.

Back to discovery call page

1. Best-client pattern review

  • Which current or past clients were the best fit?
  • What specialty were they?
  • What stage were they in?
  • What problem made them finally act?
  • What language did they use when they were ready?

2. Segment ranking

Rank physician groups into:

  • Best fit
  • Good fit
  • Low priority

Examples: dermatology, cardiology, orthopedics, anesthesiology, radiology, practice owners, near-retirement physicians.

3. Objections and hesitation list

  • Top 10 concerns physicians bring up
  • Top 10 reasons they delay
  • Top 10 questions they ask before moving forward

4. Real language collection

  • Pull phrases from emails, notes, calls, or conversations
  • Collect repeated questions prospects ask
  • Collect wording that has gotten engagement before

5. Define the first yes

  • What is the first clear next step you want from a physician?
  • Is it a short call, second-opinion review, planning session, or something else?
  • Why should that step feel natural to them?

6. ChatGPT working habit

Use ChatGPT to sharpen your expertise, not replace it. Feed it real language, real objections, real segment questions, and ask it to help clarify patterns and improve how you explain what you already know.
